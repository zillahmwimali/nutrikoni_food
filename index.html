About us

Nutrikoni is a Kenyan-based food delivery business that seeks to provide you with hearty breakfast, lunch, and dinner meals. Our meals are cooked from home, we only use organic ingredients and the food is well packed and delivered safely. Our aim is to bring tasty value and convenience right to your workplace.
We have a diverse food menu and If you are lactose intolerant or vegan or vegetarian, we have you covered as well! We cater to individual and group orders.
Some of our popular breakfast combos are such as mandazi,kaimati, chai, and sausages. For lunch and dinner, we have chapati and an array of stews, rice, pies, and salads.
